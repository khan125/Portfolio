About

Accel Sports Management founder James Krenis believes in being easy to reach and superiorly attentive to the needs of his clients. James navigates contractual and public relations matters ethically, efficiently and correctly. He was raised in New York City, graduated from the University of Memphis School of Law and Rutgers University, has lived in Tennessee since 1998 and speaks Spanish.
